## Overview

This repository is a growing collection of my CAD projects focused on real-world mechanical builds, prototypes, and functional designs.

It includes enclosures, mechanical mechanisms, custom parts, and experimental models that I design while working on personal engineering and maker projects.

Most models are created for practical use, especially in areas like electronics housing, structural components, and 3D-printable designs. Files are shared in standard formats such as STEP and STL for compatibility and easy reuse.

The goal of this repository is to document my design process, organize my work, and share non-commercial CAD projects for learning and reference.
